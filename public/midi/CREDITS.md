# Bundled hymn-tune MIDI files — source & credits

Every MIDI file in this folder comes from **The Open Hymnal Project**
(<https://openhymnal.org/>), edited by Brian J. Dumont — specifically the
2014.06 MIDI collection (`OpenHymnal2014.06-midi.zip`), downloaded June 2026.

The Open Hymnal Project releases its hymns with the copyright status of all
four copyrightable parts (music, setting, words, translation) stated in each
ABC source file. **Every file bundled here is marked "copyright: public
domain" in its Open Hymnal source**, and all project-produced content
(engraving, compilation, data files) is itself placed in the public domain by
the project.

> Note: the Open Hymnal Project assesses copyright under **United States**
> law. The tunes and settings below were additionally screened for United
> Kingdom use (life + 70 years): every composer/arranger credited died before
> 1956, or the setting is from a pre-1926 hymnal. *Down Ampney* (Ralph Vaughan
> Williams, d. 1958) was deliberately **excluded** because it remains in
> copyright in the UK until 1 January 2029, even though it is public domain in
> the US.
